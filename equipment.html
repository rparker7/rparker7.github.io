Equipment I Use

I started out using a Pentax 35 mm film camera. As digital photography became a reality and I started a family, I primarily used point and shot or compact DSLRs with a fixed lens. These include Canon and Fujifilm cameras. As mirrorless cameras have been developed and my family has started to explore more of the county, I started to research DSLRs and mirrorless camera options. Based upon my needs and price I decided to purchase a Nikon Z50 with two lens kit. The bottom line for me this was a good balance between price, technology, sensor size, and quality.
  • Nikon Z50
    • 20.9 million pixels
    • DX image sensor
    • 23.5mm X 15.7mm sensor
    • Nikon Z mount
  • Lenses
    • Nikkor Z DX 16-50mmm f/3.5-6.3 VR
    • Nikkor Z DX 50-250mm f/4.5-6.3 VR
